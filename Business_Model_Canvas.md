# Business Model Canvas

**Project: AI-Assisted Virtual Try-On and Smart Clothing Recommendation Platform**

## 1. Customer Segments
*Who your platform serves.*
- Online clothing shoppers
- Fashion-conscious young adults and students
- People unsure about clothing size while shopping online
- E-commerce clothing brands and retailers

## 2. Value Proposition
*What problem you solve and why customers will use your platform.*
- Accurate size recommendation for clothing
- Virtual try-on preview before purchase
- Personalized outfit recommendations
- Reduces wrong purchases and return rates
- Saves time by simplifying fashion choices

## 3. Channels
*How customers reach and use your service.*
- Mobile application
- Website platform
- Social media promotion
- Partnerships with e-commerce clothing brands

## 4. Customer Relationships
*How you interact with users.*
- Personalized user accounts
- AI-driven recommendations
- Customer support and feedback system
- Notifications for new styles and recommendations

## 5. Revenue Streams
*How the business earns money.*
- Affiliate commissions from clothing brands
- Premium subscription for advanced styling suggestions
- Advertising/promoted listings for fashion brands
- Brand partnership fees

## 6. Key Resources
*Important assets needed to run the platform.*
- Software platform (app/website)
- Recommendation algorithm and image processing tools
- Fashion product database
- Development team

## 7. Key Activities
*Core work required to operate the platform.*
- Developing and maintaining the platform
- Improving recommendation algorithms
- Managing brand partnerships
- Updating fashion catalogs
- Marketing and user acquisition

## 8. Key Partnerships
*External partners that help your business.*
- Online clothing retailers
- Fashion brands
- Affiliate marketing networks
- Payment and cloud service providers

## 9. Cost Structure
*Major costs involved in running the platform.*
- Software development and maintenance
- Cloud hosting and storage
- Marketing and advertising
- Staff salaries
- Platform maintenance and updates
